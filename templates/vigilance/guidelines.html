Vigilance Guidelines

The Vigilance Department plays a crucial role in maintaining transparency, integrity, and accountability within the shipyard. It ensures adherence to ethical practices, prevents misconduct, and promotes a culture of honesty among employees and stakeholders.

Objectives of Vigilance

  • Ensure compliance with legal and ethical standards.
  • Prevent and detect fraudulent activities.
  • Encourage employees to report any suspicious or unethical behavior.
  • Protect whistleblowers and maintain confidentiality.
  • Enhance operational efficiency by eliminating corruption risks.

Preventive Measures

To uphold ethical practices, the vigilance department has implemented several preventive measures, including:

  • Regular audits and inspections of operations.
  • Training programs on ethical conduct and fraud prevention.
  • Strict enforcement of anti-corruption policies.
  • Implementation of transparent procurement and contracting processes.
  • Encouragement of proactive reporting mechanisms.

How to Report a Concern?

Employees, stakeholders, and the public can report any unethical activities, misconduct, or suspicious behavior through our secure and confidential channels. Your identity will be protected, and strict action will be taken against those involved in malpractices.

Rights and Protection for Whistleblowers

We ensure full protection to whistleblowers under our policy. Any form of retaliation against individuals who report concerns in good faith will not be tolerated. If you believe you are facing any adverse action after reporting an issue, please contact our vigilance team immediately.
