Chennai Shipyard Overview

Chennai Shipyard is a premier shipbuilding and repair facility strategically located on the southeastern coast of India. Spanning over a vast area, our shipyard is equipped with cutting-edge infrastructure, including dry docks, fabrication yards, and outfitting berths. We cater to both commercial and defense sectors, ensuring world-class maritime solutions.

About Chennai Shipyard

Welcome to Chennai Shipyard, a trusted name in the maritime industry. With decades of expertise, we specialize in shipbuilding, repair, and maintenance, serving clients globally. Our state-of-the-art facilities and highly skilled workforce ensure superior quality and timely project completion.

Our mission is to deliver innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective maritime solutions. We are committed to safety, operational excellence, and environmental sustainability in all our endeavors.

Our Legacy

Established in 1985, Chennai Shipyard has evolved from a modest ship-repair facility into a globally recognized shipbuilding powerhouse. Over the years, we have successfully constructed and refurbished numerous vessels, including cargo ships, oil tankers, defense vessels, and offshore platforms, earning the trust of leading maritime organizations worldwide.

Our Expert Team

At Chennai Shipyard, our strength lies in our people. Our team comprises seasoned engineers, naval architects, skilled technicians, and industry experts dedicated to delivering excellence. Through innovation, teamwork, and technical expertise, we ensure that every project meets the highest industry standards.
