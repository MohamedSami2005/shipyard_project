About Chennai Shipyards

Welcome to Chennai Shipyards, a pioneer in India's maritime industry, renowned for excellence in shipbuilding, repairs, and marine engineering. With a legacy spanning over three decades, we have evolved into a cutting-edge shipyard, delivering superior craftsmanship, advanced technology, and innovative solutions to clients across the globe.

At Chennai Shipyards, we are driven by a vision to reshape the future of shipbuilding while adhering to the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental sustainability. From constructing high-performance vessels to providing end-to-end repair and maintenance solutions, we remain at the forefront of maritime engineering.

Our Legacy

Established in 1985, Chennai Shipyards started as a modest repair facility catering to local fishing and cargo vessels. Over the years, we expanded into **shipbuilding, offshore engineering, and naval support**, growing into one of India's most trusted maritime solution providers.

Today, with world-class infrastructure, dry docks, fabrication yards, and cutting-edge marine technology, we cater to a diverse clientele, including commercial shipping companies, defense organizations, and offshore energy firms.

Meet Our Experts

At the heart of Chennai Shipyards is a dedicated team of marine engineers, naval architects, certified welders, and industry specialists. With extensive experience in designing, constructing, and maintaining ships of various classes, our team is committed to delivering excellence with every project.

From the drawing board to the open seas, our workforce ensures precision, reliability, and superior performance in every vessel we build or repair.
